The Nathaniel Clinic is CASES’ counseling and wellness center located in our offices in Central Harlem.

We're here to help. Our treatment team includes child and adult psychiatrists, licensed social workers/therapists, and primary healthcare professionals.

The clinic serves youth (13 and older) and adults. We accept referrals from criminal justice providers, hospitals and health centers, social service providers, and members of the community. The clinic offers walk-in access on Thursday and Friday mornings.
